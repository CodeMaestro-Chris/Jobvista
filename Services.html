Our Services

We create amazing ways for you to be gainfully employed based on your field of study and eye-catching presentations and forums for you to grow your business

We do this by following
simple approach:

01. We get you employed
As a way of solving the problem of youth unemployment, we help Nigerians of different ethnicity get themselves gainfully employed by Nigerian companies and online or offline based foreign companies.

02. Business growth
Who is it for? What do they want? These are questions we help you get the answer to as an entrepreneur and we also help you boost your sales by introducing you and your business to more possible customers online.

03. Context is a difference
What do we showcase? We knit contexts together to create a perfect context for you. As an applicant or entrepreneur, you need quality concepts for a context and that's what we help you do.

  • Have a credible electronic device (e.g mobile phone, laptop, ipad, etc.).
  • Sign up with Jobvista, either on our website or mobile app.
  • Upload a compiled picture or e-file of your curriculum vitae to our email while signing up.
  • Stay up-to-date with our job recruitment messages sent either to your email or your Jobvista profile.
